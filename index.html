Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a new website?

This will heavily depend on the complexity/size of the project.
Whenever we're building a website we follow a similar flow:

  1. Create a digital wireframe that is 100% aligned with your vision and needs. (takes a few days)
  2. Create version 1 of the layout. (shouldn't take more than 10 days)
  3. Short feedback call to make sure we're on the right track. (30 minutes)
  4. Create the more complex systems on your website. This goes from custom JavaScript to really anything that you want. (Usually takes 1-3 weeks)
  5. Wrap up the project with a final feedback call.
  6. Launch your newly built website!

How much does it cost to build a website?

This is one of the most difficult questions to answer without any context but I'll try anyway! If you're looking for a very basic one-pager, you could be looking at prices around €1000. On the other hand if you want to build a multi-page website with lots of blogs, dynamic content,... then we're probably looking at €3000-€7000 investment.
