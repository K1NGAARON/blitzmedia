11 Reasons Why a Custom Website is Better Than a Template

It's no secret that in today's digital landscape, having a website is key to staying competitive. But with so many different options out there, it can be hard to decide which route to take. Should you go the do-it-yourself route and use a template? Or should you invest in a custom website? If you're on the fence about which route to take, consider these 11 reasons why a custom website is better than a template:

1. You'll have a unique website that reflects your brand.

When it comes to template websites, you're limited to the design options that are available. With a custom website, you can have a completely unique design that reflects your brand's personality and style.

For instance, if you're a luxury brand, you'll want a website that looks and feels luxurious. Template websites often have a more generic look and feel that can make your brand seem less prestigious.

Here are some of the things you can do to make sure your website design reflects your brand:

  • Use custom fonts that match your brand's style: This will help create a unique look and feel for your website.
  • Use high-quality images: Be sure to use only the best images on your website, as this will make a big difference in terms of how luxurious it looks.
  • Use custom color schemes: Template websites often have limited color options, so you may not be able to find a color scheme that perfectly matches your brand. With a custom website, you can use any colors you want to create a cohesive look.

2. You'll be able to optimize your website for SEO.

One of the most important aspects of having a successful website is making sure it's optimized for search engines. This way, when people search for keywords related to your business, your website will come up as one of the top results.

Template websites often have very limited SEO options, which can make it difficult to get your website to rank high in search engine results. With a custom website, you'll be able to work with an SEO expert to make sure your website is properly optimized.

Here are some of the things a custom website can do to help with your SEO:

  • Use the right keyword density: Keyword density is the number of times a keyword appears on a page divided by the total number of words on the page. It's important to have the right keyword density on your website so that search engines can properly index your site.
  • Use proper tag hierarchy: Tag hierarchy is the order in which tags are placed on a website. Search engines give more importance to tags that are closer to the beginning of the code.
  • Include an XML sitemap: An XML sitemap is a file that contains all of the URLs on your website. This helps search engines index your website more effectively.

3. You'll be able to create a more user-friendly website.

Template websites often have very basic features and functionality, which can make them difficult to use. With a custom website, you'll be able to add features that will make your website more user-friendly and enjoyable to use.

Imagine, for instance, that you have an online store. If your website is difficult to navigate, potential customers are likely to get frustrated and give up before making a purchase. But if your website is easy to use, they're more likely to stick around and make a purchase.

According to studies, the average user expects a website to load in two seconds or less. If your website takes longer than that to load, you're likely to lose potential customers. A custom website can be designed to load quickly, so you don't have to worry about losing potential customers.

4. You'll be able to create a more responsive website.

A responsive website is a website that automatically adjusts to the screen size of the device it's being viewed on. This is important because more and more people are using their mobile devices to access the internet.

If your website isn't responsive, it's likely that potential customers will have a difficult time viewing it on their mobile devices. This can lead to them getting frustrated and leaving your site.

With a custom website, you can make sure your site is responsive so that potential customers can have a positive experience no matter what device they're using.

5. You'll be able to create a more visually appealing website.

Let's face it: first impressions are important. And one of the first things potential customers will notice about your website is its design.

If your website has a poor design, potential customers are likely to get the impression that your business is amateurish and not worth their time. But if your website has an attractive and professional design, they're more likely to stick around and learn more about your business.

A custom website gives you the opportunity to work with a professional designer to create a website that's visually appealing and represents your brand in the best light possible.

6. You'll be able to add custom functionality to your website.

Template websites often have very limited functionality. This can be a problem if you want to add features that are specific to your business.

With a custom website, you'll be able to add any type of functionality you need. For instance, if you have an online store, you'll be able to add features like a shopping cart and payment gateway. Or if you want to collect leads, you can add a form for visitors to fill out.

7. You'll be able to make your website more search engine friendly.

If you want your website to rank high in search results, you need to make sure it's search engine friendly. And a custom website gives you the opportunity to do just that.

When you have a custom website design, you can include features that will help your website rank higher in search results. For instance, you can optimize your website for certain keywords, or you can add alt text to images.

8. You'll be able to make your website more secure.

Template websites are often not as secure as custom websites. This is because they're built using pre-made code that is often not as secure as custom code.

If you're handling sensitive information on your website, it's important to make sure your site is as secure as possible. With a custom website, you can add features that will help to keep your site and your visitors' data safe and secure.

9. You'll be able to create a website that's tailored to your specific needs.

Since template websites are pre-made, they're not always a perfect fit for every business. But with a custom website, you can make sure your site is designed specifically for your business and your target audience.

For example, if you have a specific type of customer in mind, you can design your website to appeal to them. Having a website that's tailored to your specific needs can help you stand out from the competition and attract more potential customers.

10. You'll be able to save money in the long run.

While a custom website may cost more upfront, it will actually save you money in the long run. That's because you won't have to worry about paying for expensive repairs or updates down the road.

With a template website, you may have to pay someone to make changes or fixes every time you want to make an update. But with a custom website, you can make changes and updates yourself without having to hire someone every time.

11. You'll be able to get more out of your website.

A custom website gives you the opportunity to create a site that's specifically designed for your business and your target audience. This means you'll be able to get more out of your website in terms of leads, sales, and customers.

With a template website, you're limited to the pre-made design and functionality. But with a custom website, you can create a site that's exactly what you need and that will help you achieve your business goals.

How Much Does It Cost to Build a Custom Website?

The cost of a custom website depends on a number of factors, including the size and complexity of the site, the specific features and functionality you need, and the experience of the designer or development team.

In general, you can expect to pay anywhere from $5,000 to $50,000 for a custom website. Of course, there are always exceptions to this rule, and you may be able to find a designer who charges less or more depending on your specific needs. Some of the factors that will affect the cost of your custom website include:

  • The size and complexity of the site. If you need a simple website with just a few pages, you'll probably pay on the lower end of the spectrum. But if you need a complex site with multiple features and functionality, you'll likely pay on the higher end.
  • The specific features and functionality you need. If you need a basic website with just a few pages, you'll probably pay on the lower end. But if you need a site with complex features like e-commerce or membership management, you'll likely pay on the higher end.
  • The experience of the designer or development team. If you work with a team of experienced professionals, you'll likely pay more than if you work with a less experienced team.
  • The cost of hosting and maintenance. In addition to the cost of designing and developing your website, you'll also need to factor in the cost of hosting and maintaining your site. This can range from $10 to $100 per month, depending on the size and complexity of your site.
  • The cost of additional services. In addition to the cost of design, development, hosting, and maintenance, you may also need to pay for other services like SEO or social media marketing.

As you can see, there are a lot of factors that go into the cost of a custom website. The best way to get an accurate estimate is to contact a few different design firms and ask for quotes.

When you're ready to start your custom website project, be sure to work with a team of experienced professionals who can help you create a site that's perfect for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it better to build a website from scratch or use a template?

There are pros and cons to both building a website from scratch and using a template. Building a website from scratch gives you complete control over the design and functionality of your site. However, it can be more time-consuming and expensive than using a template.

Templates can be a quick and easy way to create a website, but you may be limited in terms of customization. If you use a template, you will need to work within the framework that the template provides. This can be less flexible than building a website from scratch.

What is the difference between a custom web design and a template?

A custom web design is a completely unique design that is created specifically for your website. A template is a pre-designed layout that you can use to create your website.

What are the disadvantages of templates?

Some potential disadvantages of using templates include:

  • Limited customization options
  • May not be unique
  • Can be more expensive than building from scratch
