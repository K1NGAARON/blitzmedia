Veelgestelde vragen

Where does the tour start?

We will gather at: Grauwwerkersstraat 29, 8000 Bruges. Please be present 10 minutes before the tour departure. Should you be late, please call us at: +32(0)471 74 59 40.
Address: Grauwwerkersstraat 29, 8000 Bruges

Are last-minute reservations possible?

It's recommended to book in advance via the button 'book now'. Last-minute bookings are possible by phone: +32(0)471 74 59 40.

What kind of bikes do you offer?

We provide amazing retro bikes with a basket to store your picnic or other stuff!

What is the minimum age to take part?

For safety reasons a length of 1.5 m is required. Kids bikes or seats (up to 22 kg) can be provided via reservation. Contact us by mail hello@retrobiketours.be or by phone +32(0)471 74 59 40.

Safety

Participation is at your own risk. We regularly check the condition of our bikes. However, when renting one of our bikes, you are obliged to check if everything functions properly. During the tours, you are responsible for safety and participation in road traffic. We are not responsible for any damage. This excludes injury to life, limb and health that result from a culpable violation of obligations on our part. Drive safe and carefully!

Can we pay cash?

Yes, you have the ability to pay cash on site.
